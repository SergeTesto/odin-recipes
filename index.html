Best Dish After School

Description

This am indian dish with seasoned chicken and rice.

This meal is also served with a very tasty sauce.

Ingredients

  • chicken
  • rice
  • Onion
  • tomato
  • Garlic
  • Cream
  • Chilli pepper
  • Yoghurt
  • Coriander
  • Black pepper,Salt,Garam masala,Cumin,

Steps

  1. 1.Take the grinding ingredients in a blender and make it into a smooth puree. Set aside till use.Take the grinding ingredients in a blender and make it into a smooth puree. Set aside till use.
  2. 2.Heat oil in a pressure cooker. Add in onions, chillies and curry leaves. Saute till the onions turn golden.
  3. 3.Add in ginger garlic paste and saute for a min.
  4. 4.n goes salt and all the spice powders. Mix well for 30 sec.
  5. 5.Add in finely chopped tomatoes and saute for 5 to 7 mins till the tomatoes turn mushy.
  6. 6.Add in the chicken pieces and toss well with the masala.
  7. 7.Now cover the pressure cooker, cook for 3 whistle. Simmer the pan for 5 mins. Turn off the heat and let them steam go all by itself.
  8. 8.Open the cooker and transfer this curry to a pot.
  9. 9.Add in coconut masala and mix well. Cover this and Boil this for 10 mins or so till the curry thickens like a masala.
  10. 10.Add in coriander leaves and mix well.
  11. 11.Serve over rice.
